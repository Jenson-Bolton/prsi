# Prší

A popular Czechoslovak card game.

# How to play

The game is played with a 32-card German-suited deck (7 - Ace using a French-suited deck) and 2 or more players. Each player starts with 4 cards.

The objective is to be the first to discard all your cards.

On your turn, you must play a card that matches the suit or number of the top card on the discard pile. If you cannot make a legal play, you must draw one card from the draw pile.

The game ends the moment a player has no cards left.

## Special cards

**7 (Sedma):** Forces the next player to draw 2 cards. However, if that player holds another 7, they can play it, and the penalty passes to the next player to draw 4 cards (this stacks).

**Ace (Eso):** Skips the next player's turn. The skipped player can avoid it if they play another Ace, passing the skip to the next person.

**Queen (Svršek):**  The Queen allows you to change the active suit. You can play it on any card, and you get to declare the new suit that the next player must follow.
